Listo

A private WhatsApp assistant that turns messy shopping requests into organized lists for the whole family.

Private household utility

What is Listo?

Listo is a private, family-only WhatsApp bot built for a household in Spain. Family members send unstructured shopping requests via WhatsApp and Listo organizes everything into a clear, consolidated shopping list.

How it works

1. Message Listo
Send a WhatsApp message with what you need.
2. Listo organizes
Requests from all family members are parsed and grouped into a single shopping list.
3. Shop with ease
When it is time to shop, the list is ready, sorted, deduplicated, and complete.

Privacy

Listo is a private utility, not a commercial product. It is used exclusively by one household. No data is shared, sold, or used for marketing. No bulk or promotional messages are sent.
